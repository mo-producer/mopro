Frequently Asked Questions

What is the estimated project completion time?

Project completion time varies depending on its type and size. Reels projects usually take 1-2 business days, while promotional video projects might take 3-5 business days. More complex projects like logo animations could take 5-7 business days.

Can I request modifications to the project after delivery?

Yes, all packages include two rounds of modifications for free. Additional modifications may be charged depending on the extent of the requested changes.

What video formats can I receive?

You can receive the video in any format you need, such as MP4, MOV, AVI, and others. The video can also be exported in different resolutions to suit your needs (1080p, 4K).

Can I get the source files?

Yes, source files can be provided for an additional fee. These files include original Adobe Premiere Pro or After Effects projects.

How is payment made?

50% of the project value is paid upfront, with the remaining balance due upon final delivery. Currently, payment is available only via Western Union.
